About Artistree


Music is an international language that everyone can experience and enjoy. Artistree is all about music’s fascinatingly intertwined world. Whether it be a feature on a song, a shout out, or even a diss track resulting from a feud, Artistree has got you covered. Every time the artist entered mentions another artist in one of his or her songs, Artistry will graph that relation. This way, it is incredibly easy to visualize and picture how artists mention each other in their lyrics. Going through all of an artist’s songs and lyrics allows us to piece the puzzle together and present it in a fashionable manner. We can also see how much contact and influence an artist has over another. These artists show us that music is not a solo adventure.
